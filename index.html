Ethio robotics is upcoming leader in robotics, tackling some of the toughest robotics challenges. We combine the principles of dynamic control and balance with sophisticated mechanical designs, cutting-edge electronics, and next-generation software for high-performance robots equipped with perception, navigation, and intelligence. Ethio robotics has an extraordinary and fast-growing technical team of engineers and scientists who seamlessly combine advanced analytical thinking with bold engineering and boots-in-the-mud practicality.



Service We provide

Robot data and operations platform connecting people, process, sensors, and robots


Operate

Scale your operations with the ability to intervene, teleoperate, configure and deploy. Beam into individual robots and take control instantly.



Observe

Monitor autonomous fleets, receive timely alerts and achieve faster root cause analysis by navigating events of interest. View rich, telemetry data live and from the past. Slice massive data across several dimensions.

Analyze

Gain insights through hindsight. Improve efficiency for your customers, fleets and operators. Track trends across all aspects of your fleets and prove ROI.


Project Works

Leverage agile frameworks to provide a robust synopsis for high level overviews. Iterative approaches to corporate strategy foster collaborative thinking to further the overall value proposition.
